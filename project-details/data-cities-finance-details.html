Project information

  • Name: Análise da Situação Financeira dos municípios do Estado de Pernambuco e sua relação com indicadores sociais
  • Category: Data analysis
  • Client: Federal University of Pernambuco
  • Industry: Government
  • Project date: 2018

How a broke city budget impacts in the social indicators?

This project aimed to analyse cities from Pernambuco, Brazil that are not able to maintain its spendings with their own income (therefore need financial aid from the State) and how this would impact in the social indicators.

The main hypotesis is that the financial situation impacts directly in the performance of social indicators like education, mortality and IDHM (Human Development Index).

All the data is available at open data portals of the Tribunal de Contas de Pernambuco and Portal da Transparência. You can find the links inside the study.

Check the analysis in PDF and also the Jupyter Notebook with the code.
